Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Paint.Cross is built to be private by default. This page explains, in plain language, what data is (and is not) involved when you use this website, the Paint.Cross desktop app, and when you purchase a Pro license.

The short version

  • This website is a static site with no accounts, no cookies, and no analytics.
  • The desktop app runs entirely on your computer, requires no account, and collects no telemetry.
  • Payments are handled by Dodo Payments, our Merchant of Record. We never see your card details.

This website

paintcross.github.io is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. It has no user accounts, does not set cookies, and does not run any analytics or tracking scripts.

Two things happen locally or externally that you should know about:

  • Language preference. If you choose a display language, that choice is saved in your browser's localStorage on your own device. It never leaves your browser, and you can clear it at any time through your browser settings.
  • GitHub API requests. The site calls the public GitHub API from your browser to show current release and download information. Those requests go directly from your browser to GitHub, so your IP address reaches GitHub and GitHub's privacy statement applies to them.
Because the site is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub may log requests (such as IP addresses) for security and operational purposes, as described in the GitHub General Privacy Statement. We do not have access to, and do not collect, this data ourselves.

The desktop app

The Paint.Cross desktop app (macOS, Windows, Linux) runs locally on your computer. It does not require an account, does not collect telemetry or usage analytics, and does not upload your images or documents anywhere. Your files stay on your machine.

If you purchase Pro, activating it involves validating your license key. Activation is used only to confirm that the key is valid; it is not used to track your usage of the app.

Purchases (Pro license)

Pro purchases are processed by Dodo Payments, acting as the Merchant of Record. When you buy a Pro license:

  • Dodo Payments collects and processes your payment details and email address to complete the transaction, deliver your license key, and send your receipt.
  • We do not receive or store your card or payment details.
  • Dodo Payments' handling of your data is described in the Dodo Payments Privacy Policy.

What we don't do

  • No advertising or ad networks.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking pixels.
  • No accounts, profiles, or behavioral data.

Email

If you email us for support, we will use your email address and the contents of your message only to help you. We don't add you to mailing lists.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and its effective date. Since we collect essentially nothing, we expect changes to be rare and minor.
