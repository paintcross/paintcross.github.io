Features

Everything you need to create

Built for speed. Designed to stay out of your way.

🖌️

Rich Brush Tools

Brush, pencil, eraser, bucket fill, color picker — all the essentials with configurable size, opacity, and hardness.

📐

Layer System

Stack multiple layers with independent opacity, reorder freely, and composite them non-destructively onto the canvas.

✂️

Smart Selection

Rectangular select, freehand lasso, and magic wand with tolerance control. Copy, cut, paste, and move selections.

✍️

Text Tool

Add text with 12+ fonts, bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, outline, and drop shadow. Resize and reposition before committing.

⌨️

Keyboard Shortcuts

Every tool and action has a keyboard shortcut. Undo, redo, copy, paste, zoom — stay in the flow without touching your mouse.

💾

Open Formats

Export to PNG, open JPG, JPEG, WebP images. Save projects as .paintx to preserve layers and history.
